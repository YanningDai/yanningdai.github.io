Hi! I am a second-year PhD student working with Prof. Jürgen Schmidhuber at KAUST Center of Excellence for Generative AI. Previously, I received my M.S. and B.E. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Beihang University, advised by Prof. Shuo Gao in 2023 and 2020.

Research: My research interests span Reinforcement Learning, Robot Learning, and LLM Reasoning. I study how structured interactions between components or agents can enable more efficient, stable, and generalizable intelligence, ranging from algorithmic foundations to embodied systems.

Actively looking for research internship opportunities. Feel free to reach out for collaboration!
